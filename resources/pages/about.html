Educational History

Having completed my high school diploma, passing with a B grade, I moved on to study Computer Science at the University of the Western Cape. I achieved my degree in 2015 after studying for 3 years, completing the final year wih Cum Laude honours.

I applied to study finish my Honours (4th year) at the University of Cape Town and was accepted. I am currently studying towards this degree, leaning towards a focus in Networking and Network Security as well as dabbling in Mobile Game Development and Visualisation of Data.
